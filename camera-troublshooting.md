# Troubleshooting Camera Feed Issues

If your security camera is stuck on a loading screen or has disconnected entirely, try the following steps in order.

## Step 1: Relocate the camera
Ensure that your camera is placed within 15 feet of your Wi-Fi router. A weak wireless signal is the most common cause of feed drops. 

If moving the camera closer does not resolve the issue, proceed to the next step.

## Step 2: Check for app updates
An outdated application can cause connection errors.
1. Open the **About** menu.
2. Select **Check for Updates**.
3. Install any available updates.

## Step 3: Perform a factory reset
Only perform a factory reset if the previous steps did not fix the issue. 

*Warning: A factory reset deletes all of your saved settings and custom configurations.*

1. Locate the physical button on the back of the camera.
2. Press and hold the button for 10 seconds.
3. Release the button and wait for the camera to restart.
